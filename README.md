🧠 MindLog - AI-Powered Mental Wellness Analytics Platform
https://mindlog1.onrender.com/

📌 Overview

MindLog is an AI-powered mental wellness platform that helps users track and understand their emotional well-being through journaling. Users can write daily journal entries, receive AI-generated emotional analysis, interact with a personalized AI therapy chatbot, and visualize emotional trends over time through interactive dashboards.

The platform combines Generative AI, analytics, and visualization to provide actionable insights into users' mental health journeys.

🚀 Features
✍️ AI Journal Analysis
Users can create and store daily journal entries.
Gemini AI analyzes each entry to detect emotions and sentiment.
Supports identification of emotions such as:
Happiness
Sadness
Anxiety
Anger
Stress
Motivation

🤖 Personalized AI Therapy Chatbot
Context-aware chatbot powered by Gemini AI.
Retains previous journal context to provide more personalized conversations.
Delivers supportive responses based on the user's emotional history.

📊 Emotion Analytics Dashboard
Interactive Plotly visualizations.
Emotion trend tracking over time.
Emotional distribution analysis.
Daily and weekly wellness insights.

🔥 Streak Tracking
Tracks journaling consistency.
Encourages healthy journaling habits through streak monitoring.

📄 Weekly Wellness Reports
AI-generated summaries of emotional patterns.
Highlights improvements and recurring concerns.
Provides personalized wellness suggestions.

👤 User Authentication
Secure user registration and login.
Individual journal histories and analytics.
